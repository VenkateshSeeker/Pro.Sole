Our Story


ProSole was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to protect your shoes and keep them looking their best, no matter where you go. It all started when we noticed how frustrating it was to step on bubble gum, dirt, or anything that could ruin our favorite shoes. We knew there had to be a better way to keep shoes clean and stylish.

Our Solution


Introducing the ProSole Detachable Sole Protector—a revolutionary product made from eco-friendly pineapple skin. It’s designed to effortlessly snap onto the sole of your shoes, creating a protective barrier against dirt, gum, and grime. When you’re done, simply peel it off, and your shoes remain as pristine as when you first put them on.

Why ProSole?


We believe that your shoes deserve the best care, especially when they represent your style, comfort, and confidence. ProSole isn’t just a product; it’s a statement. It’s for those who value their footwear and want to keep it looking fresh and new. Whether you’re protecting an expensive pair of sneakers or your everyday favorites, ProSole has got you covered.
1. Eco-Friendly: Made from sustainable pineapple skin, ProSole is kind to the planet.
2. Easy to Use: Snap on, snap off—keeping your shoes clean has never been easier.
3. Perfect for Gifting: A thoughtful gift for shoe lovers, sneakerheads, and anyone who takes pride in their footwear.

Our Mission


At ProSole, our mission is to combine innovation with sustainability, creating products that make life easier while caring for the environment. We’re committed to providing high-quality, practical solutions that protect not just your shoes, but also our planet.
